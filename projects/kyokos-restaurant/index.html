About

At Kyoko's, the richness of imported Japanese wood and the sleek contemporary decor have the same stylish edge as the food. Step inside and settle into our state-of-the-art 20-seat sushi bar or sit in a cozy modernized tatami-style booth for one of the most extraordinary dining experiences in South Florida.
